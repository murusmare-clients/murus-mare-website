01
About

A website is something like a wall. It stands between your business and the world, and it has to hold.

The name comes from a 12th-century Arab chronicler describing Jijel: an ancient city on the sea, with an old wall that extends into the water. Phoenicians built it. Romans reinforced it. Byzantines held it. The Ottomans took it. Two thousand years of salt have weathered the rest.

Most of it is still there.

Small business owners should not have to think about their website every week. They should be serving customers, quoting jobs, booking appointments, and running the place. The site can be a quiet thing in the background that stays updated, stays trustworthy, and makes it easier for people to contact you.

That is the work we like doing.
